What is Bhoomify?

Bhoomify is a platform where students earn points for eco-friendly actions like waste segregation, planting trees, and reducing plastic use.

1

Perform Action

Segregate waste or plant a sapling.

2

Get Verified

Show your action to a faculty member.

3

Claim Points

Faculty adds points to your Bhoomify ID.
